About Me



Greetings, humans. I know that this old high school picture of me probably looks like I'm saying, "hey, dude", but I'm really not. Back then, having bleached hair and sideburns was a thing - not so much now.

I will say that hittin' that top button is always fashionable, no matter the decade - unless you're Henry Rollins. What the neck!

Some of my interests include artwork, soccer, biking, skateboarding, running around in the mountains, and melting faces with my guitar. I've been interested in coding for a while, and have written small apps independently here and there over the years. I was introduced to Web development a couple of years ago and I'm really excited to finally get the formal training I've wanted.
